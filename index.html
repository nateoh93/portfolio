About me

I am a former CPA turned investment banking professional turned software engineer. My passion for challenging myself, solving complex problems, and continuous learning and development have ultimately led me to pursue software engineering as a career.

As a web developer, I enjoy the creativity and the challenge the field brings as well as the opportunity to always be learning. I take satisfaction in seeing the direct results of the work I put in, and I look forward to working with people much smarter than me to build products that have a lasting impact in the community.

In my free time I enjoy traveling, CrossFit, watching UFC, NBA and NFL, and spending time with friends and family. This year I bought an Epic pass, so you'll most likely find me carving the slopes at Tahoe!
